Representing the 4th District of Tennessee

Scott DesJarlais

Scott DesJarlais is the representative for Tennessee’s 4th congressional district and is a Republican. He has served since Jan 5, 2011. DesJarlais is next up for reelection in 2020.

Committee Membership

Scott DesJarlais sits on the following committees:

  • House Committee on Agriculture
    • Member, Subcommittee on Livestock and Foreign Agriculture
    • Member, Subcommittee on Nutrition, Oversight, and Department Operations
  • House Committee on Armed Services
    • Member, Subcommittee on Intelligence and Emerging Threats and Capabilities
    • Member, Subcommittee on Strategic Forces
